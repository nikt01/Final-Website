History Of The Blue Code Company

Lets Begin With Our History Then.
The story of The Blue Code Company began with a love of technology and the belief that everyone, regardless of country or region, should have access to modern computer solutions.
The company's founders, two brothers Adrian and Matthew Blue, lovers of technological innovations and computer enthusiasts, decided to combine their passions and experience in the IT industry.

Adrian, a hardware expert specializing in the latest computer components, had always dreamed of bringing fast and efficient computers to people in different parts of the world.

Matthew, on the other hand, was a talented entrepreneur with experience in international sales.

The Blue brothers started their business with a small online store, offering high-quality computer components. Over time, their commitment and reliability in customer service earned them the trust of customers from various countries such as Poland, England, France, Bulgaria, Indonesia and Saudi Arabia.

Soon after, The Blue Code Company established partnerships with many reputable computer stores and retail chains, such as Media Expert, X-kom, Media Markt, RTV Euro AGD, Amazon, Best Buy and Mouser Electronics.

This broad network has allowed the company to reach even more customers around the world. Always open to new opportunities, the company also decided to offer customized services, such as computer upgrade consulting and comprehensive computer kits tailored to various applications.

The Blue Code Company has quickly become a recognizable IT Company in the international market, with a history of passion for technology, customer trust and openness to cooperation.
With a constant focus on quality and an innovative approach to business, the company has become not only a place to buy computer hardware, but also a partner for many individuals and companies from all corners of the world.
